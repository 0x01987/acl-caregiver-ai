\# CareGuide AI - Caregiver Personas



\## Persona 1: Family Caregiver Supporting a Parent with Dementia



\### Profile



\* Name: Susan

\* Age: 58

\* Relationship: Daughter

\* Care Setting: Home

\* Care Recipient: Mother with early-stage dementia



\### Challenges



\* Difficulty understanding discharge instructions

\* Managing medications across multiple providers

\* Organizes follow-up activities
