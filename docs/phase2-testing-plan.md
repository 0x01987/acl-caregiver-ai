\# CareGuide AI - Phase 2 Testing Plan



\## Goal



Evaluate the effectiveness of CareGuide AI in real-world caregiver environments.



\---



\## Participants



Target:



\* 10–20 family caregivers



Potential Care Settings:



\* Post-hospital discharge

\* Dementia care

\* Chronic disease management

\* Aging in place



\---



\## Testing Activities



Participants will:



1\. Upload healthcare documents

2\. Review AI-generated care plans

3\. Use daily task tracking

4\. Record symptoms and observations

5\. Manage follow-up actions

6\. Generate family care summaries



\---



\## Evaluation Criteria



\### Accuracy



Assess whether extracted information matches source documents.



\### Usability



Assess ease of use and navigation.



\### Burden Reduction



Assess whether caregivers spend less time interpreting healthcare information.



\### Adoption



Assess willingness to continue using the platform.



\---



\## Iterative Improvement



Findings from testing will be used to:



\* Improve AI prompts

\* Improve dashboard usability

\* Refine caregiver workflows

\* Enhance accessibility features



\---



\## Future Interoperability



Future phases will explore integration with healthcare systems using standards such as Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources (FHIR) and other industry-supported healthcare data exchange standards.
