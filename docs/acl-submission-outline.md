# CareGuide AI – ACL Caregiver AI Challenge Submission Outline

## 1. Problem Statement

Family caregivers are frequently responsible for managing complex healthcare information, including hospital discharge instructions, medication lists, care plans, provider recommendations, and follow-up requirements.

Many caregivers have little or no formal healthcare training and often struggle to understand medical terminology, organize care activities, monitor symptoms, and coordinate information with family members and healthcare providers.

These challenges increase caregiver burden, contribute to stress and burnout, and can lead to missed medications, missed appointments, and delayed recognition of warning signs.

CareGuide AI was designed to help reduce this burden by transforming complex healthcare documents into clear, caregiver-friendly action plans and providing tools that support ongoing care coordination.

---

## 2. Caregiver Need

Through review of caregiver challenges identified by ACL and existing caregiver support literature, several recurring needs were identified:

* Simplifying complex healthcare documents
* Understanding medication instructions
* Tracking daily care activities
* Monitoring symptoms and observations
* Coordinating care among family members
* Managing follow-up appointments
* Reducing caregiver stress and cognitive burden

CareGuide AI directly addresses these needs through AI-assisted document interpretation and caregiver workflow support.

---

## 3. Proposed Solution

CareGuide AI is an AI-powered caregiver support platform that converts healthcare documents into structured, caregiver-friendly care plans.

The platform provides:

### AI Care Plan Generation

* Plain-language summaries
* Medication extraction
* Daily care tasks
* Warning sign identification
* Follow-up recommendations

### Caregiver Workflow Support

* Daily task checklist
* Follow-up tracker
* Symptom and observation tracking
* Caregiver notes
* Care timeline

### Family Care Coordination

* Family care summaries
* Shareable caregiver updates
* Care history tracking

### AI Caregiver Assistant

A context-aware assistant that helps caregivers understand care information, review tasks, and organize care activities while maintaining human oversight and medical safety guardrails.

---

## 7. AI Safety and Human Oversight

CareGuide AI follows a human-in-the-loop approach.

The system:

* Supports caregiver decision-making
* Does not provide medical diagnoses
* Does not recommend treatment changes
* Does not replace clinicians
* Clearly communicates limitations

Caregivers remain responsible for reviewing information and consulting healthcare professionals when appropriate.

---

## 8. Privacy, Transparency, and Trust

CareGuide AI is designed around ACL's Caregiver AI principles.

Key commitments include:

* Minimal data collection
* Transparent AI-generated outputs
* Clear disclaimers and limitations
* Respect for caregiver and care recipient autonomy
* Human review of AI-generated recommendations

The platform promotes informed decision-making rather than automated decision-making.

---

## 11. Expected Impact

CareGuide AI is expected to:

* Reduce caregiver burden
* Improve understanding of healthcare information
* Increase completion of care tasks and follow-up actions
* Improve communication among caregivers
* Support aging in place
* Promote safer and more coordinated home-based care

By simplifying complex healthcare information and providing practical caregiver support tools, CareGuide AI helps caregivers spend less time managing paperwork and more time supporting the people they care for.
