\# CareGuide AI - User Stories



\## Understanding Care Plans



As a caregiver,



I want discharge instructions translated into simple language



So that I understand how to care for my loved one at home.



\---



\## Medication Management



As a caregiver,



I want medications extracted from healthcare documents



So that I can administer medications accurately.



\---



\## Task Management



As a caregiver,



I want a checklist of daily care activities



So that I can track completion and reduce missed tasks.



\---



\## Symptom Tracking



As a caregiver,



I want to record symptoms and observations



So that I can identify patterns and communicate concerns to providers.



\---



\## Follow-Up Coordination



As a caregiver,



I want follow-up actions identified automatically



So that I do not miss appointments or provider instructions.



\---



\## Family Communication



As a caregiver,



I want a shareable care summary



So that family members remain informed about care activities and status.



\---



\## Documentation



As a caregiver,



I want notes and care events recorded in a timeline



So that I can maintain an accurate history of care activities.
