## Abstract (250 Words Maximum)

CareGuide AI is an AI-powered caregiver support platform designed to reduce caregiver burden by transforming complex healthcare documents into clear, actionable care plans. Family caregivers are frequently responsible for interpreting hospital discharge instructions, medication schedules, follow-up appointments, and symptom monitoring guidance without formal clinical training. This often leads to confusion, missed care tasks, medication errors, and increased stress.

CareGuide AI enables caregivers to upload discharge summaries, medication lists, care plans, and other healthcare documents. Using document extraction, OCR processing, and generative AI, the platform converts complex medical information into caregiver-friendly summaries, medication schedules, daily care tasks, follow-up reminders, warning signs, and care coordination recommendations.

The solution also includes an interactive AI Caregiver Assistant, symptom tracking, caregiver notes, family care summaries, care timelines, care status reports, and caregiver shift handoff reports that support communication among family members and other caregivers. Accessibility features, including Large Text Mode and High Contrast Mode, help ensure usability across diverse caregiver populations.

CareGuide AI is designed to support human decision-making rather than replace it. The platform emphasizes transparency, caregiver empowerment, safety, accessibility, and burden reduction while helping caregivers spend less time organizing information and more time supporting their loved ones.

The current prototype exceeds Technology Readiness Level (TRL) 3 and demonstrates a functioning end-to-end workflow using AI-assisted document analysis and caregiver support tools.
